Resources for Sexual Assault Survivors

According to statistics, many women suffer from sexual assault. Survivors of assault deserve to know that they are not alone. Plus, there is a support system and local resources that can help overcome the trauma. Here is a list of links to various agencies that provides counseling, legal aid, or advocacy for sexual assault.

Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network (RAINN)

RAINN is the nation's largest anti-sexual violence organization. RAINN created and operates the National Sexual Assault Hotline in partnership with more than 1,000 local sexual assault service providers across the country. Call them toll-free at (800) 656-HOPE(4673). Or chat at https://www.rainn.org/

Texas Association Against Sexual Assault (TAASA)

TAASA is the unifying voice to eliminate sexual violence in Texas. As the statewide coalition of survivors, advocates, rape crisis centers, and allied professionals, we are committed to fostering a culture that respects the fundamental rights and dignity of all. https://taasa.org/

Legal Aid for Survivors of Sexual Assault (LASSA)

LASSA is a statewide, collaborative network of legal service organizations working to ensure survivors have access to critical civil legal services throughout Texas. The network provides free legal education, advice, representation, and other resources. https://lassatexas.org/

Safe Horizon

Safe Horizon is the nation’s leading victim assistance organization, operating a network of programs across New York City communities and systems. We work with survivors of all forms of violence, including racism, to move from crisis to confidence. Need help? Call our 24-hour hotline (llámenos para ayudarle) 1-800-621-HOPE (4673). https://www.safehorizon.org/

RESILIENCE

Resilience is an independent, not-for-profit organization dedicated to the healing and empowerment of sexual assault survivors through non-judgmental crisis intervention counseling, individual and group trauma therapy, and medical and legal advocacy in the greater Chicago metropolitan area. https://www.ourresilience.org/

Pennsylvania Coalition Against Rape (PCAR)

PCAR partners with a network of rape crisis center programs to bring help, hope, and healing around issues of sexual violence to all 67 counties in Pennsylvania. All programs provide, at a minimum, basic services to victims/survivors of sexual violence, rape, and incest including free and confidential crisis counseling, prevention education programs, services for the victim’s family, friends, partners, or spouses, & information and referrals to other services in your area. Call 1-888-772-7227 https://pcar.org/

Advocating Agencies to Support

Project Beloved

Project Beloved strives to educate, advocate, and collaborate to change the conversation about sexual assault and empower survivors to find their voices. https://www.projectbeloved.org/

Joy Heart Foundation

The Joyful Heart Foundation is a national organization based in New York City. Our mission is to transform society’s response to sexual assault, domestic violence, and child abuse, support survivors’ healing, and end this violence forever. https://www.joyfulheartfoundation.org/

End the Backlog Project

The End the Backlog project has shed light on the dismaying scope of the backlog, the incontrovertible urgency of testing rape kit evidence, and the political changes necessary to achieve comprehensive rape kit reform. https://www.endthebacklog.org/
